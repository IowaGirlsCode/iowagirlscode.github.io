What is Iowa Girls Code?

In February 2016, we founded the first ever coding camp for high school girls in Iowa. The one-day event was a successful start, and now it's time to bring Silicon Valley level tech education to Silicon Prairie.
Iowa Girls Code is a program that matches high school girls with mentors at local tech companies and teaches the basics of computer science and app development.
